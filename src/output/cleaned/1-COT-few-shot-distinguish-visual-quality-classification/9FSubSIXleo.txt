Visual Key Event 1(visual): The instructor begins the yoga session with a deep squat, emphasizing the alignment of the spine and the energy flow from the tailbone to the crown of the head.
Visual Key Event 2(visual): The instructor guides the participant to lengthen and straighten the spine while maintaining a squat position.
Visual Key Event 3(visual): The participant adjusts their feet into a V position and opens their hips, maintaining an oppositional pull between the tailbone and the crown of the head.
Visual Key Event 4(visual): The participant sinks deeper into the squat, using their elbows to push against their inner thighs for support.
Visual Key Event 5(visual): The participant transitions from the squat to a seated position, flexing their feet and lowering their pelvis.
Visual Key Event 6(visual): The participant moves into the butterfly pose, pressing their thumbs into their groins and lifting their chest.
Visual Key Event 7(visual): The participant deepens the butterfly pose by hinging at the hip flexors and tilting forward.
Visual Key Event 8(visual): The participant maintains the butterfly pose, ensuring rootedness through the sit bones and length through the spine.
Visual Key Event 9(visual): The participant transitions into the cone pose, either holding their big toes or pointing their thumbs up to open the main meridians of the body.
Visual Key Event 10(visual): The participant deepens the cone pose, releasing down while trying to keep the sit bones glued to the floor.
Visual Key Event 11(visual): The participant maintains the cone pose, focusing on a neutral pelvic tilt and rooting the sit bones.
Visual Key Event 12(visual): The participant comes out of the poses and shakes out their legs, marking the end of the yoga series.
